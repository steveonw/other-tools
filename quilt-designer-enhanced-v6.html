How to use

  • Choose a color from the palette or the color picker.
  • Solid: paints a full square.
  • HST: paints the triangle you click. Right‑click a diagonal cell to toggle its direction.
  • QST: paints one of 4 triangles. Right‑click a QST cell to rotate it.
  • Copy: click anywhere in the editor to copy the current block.
  • Paste: click anywhere in the editor to paste the clipboard into the current block. Use ↻/↔/↕ to transform the clipboard first.
  • Undo/Redo: works for paint strokes and paste.
  • Auto‑save: your work is saved in this browser automatically.

Note: Export PNG exports the entire 36×36 design at high resolution.
